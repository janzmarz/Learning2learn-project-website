Interactive test checklist

The purpose of this checklist as mentioned in the course is to ensure that you are prepared for any upcoming test you may have, as stated by Richard Felder, PhD "The answer the the question "How should I prepare for the test?" Is -- Do Whatever it takes to be able to answer "Yes"(meaning "usually") to most of the questions on this list."

I have added 2 additional boxes if you have any ideas of your own that you would like to add to the list, try it out!
